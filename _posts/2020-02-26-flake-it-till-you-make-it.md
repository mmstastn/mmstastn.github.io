The natural world is a feast of fluid mechanics. This picture is from the Jokulsarlon Lagoon in Iceland.  The interplay of floating ice that is deforming and melting, and the changing light from the crazy Iceland weather leads to so many colours and shapes you can spend all day just looking around.
